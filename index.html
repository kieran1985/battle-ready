ABOUT ME

DANNY MISSIN

Hello and welcome to Battle Ready Fitness! ​
After nearly 15 years in high level competitive sports and over 8 years working as a personal trainer I'm able to bring you Battle Ready Fitness, a website dedicated to getting you to where you want to be without cutting corners for sustainable, life changing results. ​
I've had the pleasure of working alongside some of the worlds leading fitness professionals as a student, an athlete and even as a colleague, learning everyday to separate the myths from the science to bring you the best training available and now for the first time available online direct to your device. ​
So your journey to the YOU that you dream of starts here, so... ARE YOU BATTLE READY?

1-2-1 Personal Training

One-to-ones are a great way to get the motivation you need. Whether you are a gym pro looking to learn new skills and techniques, or a complete beginner wanting to learn in a supportive and safe environment, one-to-one work with a coach could be for you.
If you would like to find out more enquire below. And don't worry, if your confidence isn't where you'd like it to be yet, you can request a time when there will be nobody else in the studio.

Online Training

Online PT is a great tool for people who are on the go and cannot commit to a one-to-one appointment. There's no secret as to why 24 hour gyms are popping up all over the place - because work life can be hectic!
With online PT you will have all the personalised programs, nutrition plans and support you need right to an app on your device, as well as an open line of communication to me in-app. To find out more, enquire below.

Boxing/Kickboxing/Wrestling/MMA

As an ex-professional Mixed Martial Artist I can provide one-to-one or small group training for anyone wanting to learn combat sports. I have a bunch of members that do it for fitness and fun but can also offer strict tuition for aspiring competitors to established professionals. To find out more enquire below

Buddy training and small groups

I also offer buddy training and small group training upon request. So if you’re looking to get a great weekly workout with the support of your friends, enquire now to find out more.

Nutrition plans

Nutrition is the foundation of any fitness plan. Without it you’ll feel stronger, fitter, but you won’t see the results. Whatever your preference, vegan, kosher, weight loss or nutrition for sports performance I’ve got it covered with the most in-depth nutrition analysis and plans.
